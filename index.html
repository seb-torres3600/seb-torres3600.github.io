Logos Jiu Jitsu

20 Nov 2021

Work In Progress

Logos Jiu Jitsu, is the academy where I train. If you're ever in Denver it is the place to be, checkout their website for more information. The academy is relatively new so most of its operations are done on pen and paper.

As a fun project, I decided to create a IOS app that would allow the business aspect of a martial arts academy to be a lot easier.

What it does?

The app makes the business aspect of an academy easier. It has the ability to sign in, once logged in you get the ability to add new members to the academy, check in people, and retrieve the information of members.

What is it built with?

IOS apps have the ability to made in Swift or Objective-C. With this being my first attempt at building a mobile app I decided that Swift would make my life easier. I needed a way to securely hold information, with maybe potentially wanting to retrieve this information someday on a website once the business gets scaled up more so I decided to go with the Firebase Database and it was free oppossed to Apple's CloudKit. The sign-in authentication is also handled by Firebase to give access to the application.

Features


- Sign Up New members

- Allow Members to Check In for Class

- Get a List of Members

- Retrieve Important Member Info

- Get Average Number of Members per Class for the Month

- Get Number of Members per Class for the Day

Features Being Worked On


- Create PDF Contract from New Member Info

- Make Offline Use of App

- Make Code Easier to Maintain

- Add Profile Photo for Members

- Adjust Database Rules
